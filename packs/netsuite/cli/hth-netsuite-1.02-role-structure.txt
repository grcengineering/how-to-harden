# HTH NetSuite Control 1.2: Implement Role-Based Access Control
# Profile: L1 | NIST: AC-3, AC-6
# https://howtoharden.com/guides/netsuite/#12-implement-role-based-access-control

# HTH Guide Excerpt: begin cli-role-structure
Roles:
├── Administrator (limit to 2-3 users)
├── Financial Controller
│   └── Full financial access, reporting
├── Accounts Payable
│   └── Vendor bills, payments (no GL access)
├── Accounts Receivable
│   └── Customer invoices, receipts
├── Sales Representative
│   └── Quotes, orders (no financial reports)
└── Integration Role
    └── API access for specific integrations
# HTH Guide Excerpt: end cli-role-structure
